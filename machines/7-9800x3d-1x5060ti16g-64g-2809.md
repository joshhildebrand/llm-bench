## Verdict on the second 5060 Ti (even on a x1 slot)

Its entire value is **making 17–27 GB models fully resident**: +1.6× on a model that fits one card
anyway (Q2 35B), +2.1–2.5× on Q4-class 30–35B MoEs, +3.2× on a dense 27B, and ~2.7× on aggregate
serving throughput. The x1 link is a non-issue for resident layer-split (only activations cross).
Moved to another machine, this card alone caps at ~14 GB quants resident (Q2-class 35B at 64 tok/s,
9–13 GB Q4 mid-size models) — a respectable second box, but this box loses its 100+ tok/s class.
Keep the pair if 30–35B Q4 @ 100+ tok/s is the daily driver; split if two independent ~60 tok/s
boxes are worth more than one fast one.

Single-card sweet spots if the split happens: qwen3.6-35B **Q2_K_XL resident, 64 tok/s / 1150
prefill** (verify Q2 quality for your use) or coder-30B IQ4_XS light-hybrid at 57 / 1286.
